About us

Based in Bodmin, Cornwall, we are a family-run club dating back to the Cornico-Devornian wars, where our grandfathers honourably lost to the superior Devornian might and sobriety. To this day we pride ourselves in teaching adults and children of all ages and ability to accurately glance off the edge of the target, treating our participation in regional and national archery contests as one might hope to score in a game of golf; low.

"I love to let my hair down and my arrows fly on the weekend. I have made so many friends since joining the club and I'd recommend anyone to come and join!"

-Joyce P. - Company secretary / frequent archer since 2019
